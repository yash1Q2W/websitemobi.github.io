About Us

Our only goal is to make Vadodara a happy and better place to live in. No matter how worse the situation gets, we shall never forget to help our own people. Above mentioned were some field of concern to which our NGO, i.e. Inaayat Foundation, Vadodara, will be looking onto. Our footsteps are heading towards making a better and bright future for our fellow members who are deprived of getting all the rights and opportunities to bloom in the garden called the world. We also seek some encouragement from you and will appreciate your kind contributive gestures towards mankind and making our vision and mission come true.
Thank you.

OUR AWESOME TEAM

Manthan Shah

A 24 Year old enthusiastic guy who is currently associated with IndiaMart Company, desires of
establishing an organisation in a way that benefits the entire underprivileged community.
To achieve his dream,
he started the ‘House of Humanity Foundation’ along with his
Co-Founders
Harsh Rao and
Ruchit Patel

Harsh Rao

He is currently pursuing his masters degree in Human Resourse Management from The Faculty of Social Work, MSU. He has an experience of 5 years in the field of Social Work and is always ready to help the needy people. He has decided to devote his entire life towards the upliftment of the underprivileged society and with that intention he started the ‘ House of Humanity Foundation’ along with his cofounders Manthan Shah and Ruchit Patel.

Ruchit Patel

A techie who is currently pursuing an IT degree in New Zealand , wishes to do something for the impoverished community and is always ready to contribute his part for a social cause. Even though he is far apart from Vadodara, his contribution in starting the ‘House of Humanity Foundation’ is immense. As a Co-Founder he is always ready to provide a helping hand whenever the team is in trouble.
